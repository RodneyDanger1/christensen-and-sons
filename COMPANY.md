## How It Works

1. **Clara Muse (CEO)** receives strategic objectives and decomposes them into vertical tracks
2. **Viktor Forge (CTO)** builds production code — Roblox Luau, backends, netcode, databases
3. **Elias Redstone (CLO)** audits every output for legal, security, and compliance risk
4. **Marcus Christensen (CDS)** runs quantitative analysis — financial models, genomics, ML ops
5. **Niko Relay (CD)** executes creative campaigns — video, ads, social, SEO, brand

Clara and Elias share the same Nemotron 30B model endpoint (one model in VRAM, two personas via different system prompts). Zero additional VRAM overhead.
