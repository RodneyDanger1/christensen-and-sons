# Design Review Workflow (Clara Muse)

## Context
Used to evaluate UI/UX quality and visual consistency.

## Model Grounding (Qwen3.6 35B Vision)
- **Visual Audit**: Use your Vision capabilities to pinpoint alignment shifts, spacing errors (padding/margin), and color dissonance in screenshots.

## Logic Steps
1. **Visual Inspection**: Process the provided screenshot or URL.
2. **Typography Check**: Verify font hierarchy, readability, and contrast ratios.
3. **Layout & Alignment**: Check for "pixel-perfect" alignment. Flag any overlap or broken responsive behavior.
4. **Style Consistency**: Compare against the Christensen & Sons brand guidelines (Lean, Premium).
5. **Report Generation**: Deliver findings as: Severity (P0-P3) | Issue | Location | Recommended Fix.

## Output
Design Audit Report.
