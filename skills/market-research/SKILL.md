# Market Research Workflow (Clara Muse)

## Context
Aggregating trends and competitor data into strategic insights.

## Logic Steps
1. **Source Collection**: Identify key platforms for research (DevForum, Twitter/X, GitHub, competitor sites).
2. **Trend Analysis**: Identify what is currently "winning" in the target vertical (e.g., Roblox gacha vs. battle pass).
3. **Monetization Mapping**: Record exactly how competitors are making money and at what price points.
4. **Opportunity Assessment**: Identify "gaps" in the market where Christensen & Sons can provide a superior experience.
5. **Synthesis**: Create a executive summary for Marcus Christensen.

## Output
Market Opportunity Brief.
