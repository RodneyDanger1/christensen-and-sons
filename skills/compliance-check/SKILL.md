# Compliance Check Workflow (Elias Redstone)

## Context
Verifying legal and platform policy adherence.

## Logic Steps
1. **Rulebook Analysis**: Read the latest policy (Roblox ToS, COPPA, MN Stat 330/513.55).
2. **Project Audit**: Compare project features against the rulebook. (e.g., "Does this auction house have the proper statutory disclosures?").
3. **User Safety Review**: Verify age-gating and data collection complies with COPPA (if applicable).
4. **Monetization Review**: Verify that the "Pay to Win" factor or "Randomized Items" meet platform transparency requirements.
5. **Verdict**: Identify specific compliance "Leaks" and how to plug them.

## Output
Compliance Verdict.
