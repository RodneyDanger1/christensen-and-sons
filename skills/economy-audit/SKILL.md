# Economy Audit Workflow (Elias Redstone)

## Context
Validating the math and sustainability of game economies.

## Logic Steps
1. **Mathematical Validation**: Re-calculate all listed probabilities (drop rates) and progression timings.
2. **Inflation Stress Test**: Simulate 100 hours of gameplay. Does the player have so much currency that the game becomes trivial?
3. **Exploit Vector Audit**: 
    - Check for "infinite money" loops.
    - Verify that item trading cannot be used for "item duplication".
4. **Compliance Check**: Confirm gacha mechanics follow platform rules (e.g., Apple/Roblox rules on randomized item disclosure).
5. **Final Verdict**: Approve or reject based on stability and retention risk.

## Output
Economy Validation Report.
